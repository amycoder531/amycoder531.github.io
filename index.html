Duck Egg

Posted on 3/25/2020

Today we found an abandoned duck egg. We decided to take it home and hatch it as an experiment. We did some research of temperatures to hatch the egg at. The temperature to hatch an egg is 37.5 degrees Celcius or 99.5 degrees Fahrenheit.
